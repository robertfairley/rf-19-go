A brief bit about myself

I'm a developer in Toronto recently working in media designing and building tooling for production and diagnostics in digital publishing and radio. I work largely in Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, NodeJS, React, and C/C++. I'm interested in working in scientific and hardware programming—and I spend much of my hobby time learning in those fields. My scientific interests center most strongly around physics— but I'm still working refining that one.

Most of my time otherwise is spent engineering software for publishing brands at St Joseph Communications (formerly Rogers Media), but I also like reading, playing music, cooking, getting exercise, and playing the odd video game. At one time I dedicated a lot of time to playing music, much of the time in a club who's music manager reportedly tried to pay the bands with pairs of blue jeans—at our rates the pants were probably worth more, but it was still a good time.

I grew up in a small town and love the outdoors. Regrettably I'm inside most of the time these days, but I've been hoping to get to Nahanni National Park for some time, and I look forward to setting myself up somewhere, sometime where I can be outdoors a lot more.

There's a bit more to my past, and I'm hopeful for the future. For either of those, I think we'd be better off talking .
