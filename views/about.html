A brief bit about myself

I'm a developer in Toronto currently working in media designing and building tooling for production and diagnostics in digital publishing and radio. I work largely in Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, NodeJS, React, and C. I'm interested in working in scientific and hardware programming—and I spend much of my hobby time learning in those fields. My scientific interests center most strongly around physics— but I'm still working refining that one.

Most of my time otherwise is spent engineering software for publishing and radio brands at Rogers Media, but I also like reading, cooking, getting exercise, and playing the odd video game. I spent some time playing music, much of the time in a club who's music manager reportedly tried to pay the bands with pairs of blue jeans—at our rates the pants were probably worth more, but it was a good time. (Segue into a favourite clip of a favourite band of mine, but I recommend the whole thing)

I grew up in a small town and love the outdoors. Regrettably I'm inside most of the time these days, but I've been hoping to get to Nahanni National Park for some time.

There's a bit more to my past, and I'm hopeful for the future. For either of those, I think we'd be better off talking .
